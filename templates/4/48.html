If I could have one wish, it would be for a new way to enjoy fried potatoes

What about French fries? Hash browns? Potato chips? Home fries? Tater tots? Potato pancakes? Seasoned fries? Waffle fries? Poutine? What else could possibly be left?

Unlike you, I refuse to accept any limits to what the fried potato is capable of.
