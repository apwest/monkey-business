Hey Monte, what's the hardest thing you've ever done?

 
 
 

Hmm. That's a good question. I guess the hardest thing I've ever done is say goodbye to someone I really loved. The timing just wasn't right and we both knew it, and we figured if it was meant to be, we'd come back together in the end. There's that famous saying, "If you love somebody, let them go, for if they return, they were always yours. If they don't, they never were." I really took that to heart. It's tough sometimes. I still miss her. But it was for the best. How about you? What's the hardest thing you've ever done?

 

Ugh. I was going to say, "Lick my own eyeball," but having to listen to that story just now beats that for sure.
