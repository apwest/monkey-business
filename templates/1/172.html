Original release date: Sun, 25 Dec 2011
 

Mortimer! Why are you wheeling that thing up my sidewalk?
 

It's your present! No stupid injunction is going to keep me away!

  
 
 

I told you, if you tried to dump that broken photocopier on my property, I'd call the police! I don't care how beautifully it's wrapped!
 

Will you just shut up and open it?

  
 
 

Fine, but only so the cops can see- hey, wait, it's a box. What's in here? Wha- two Segways! Inscribed with our names! So we can ride together! Mortimer, this is the most incredible gift ever!
 

And you almost ruined it. Merry Christmas, you crotchety old stick-in-the-mud. Now let's ride!
