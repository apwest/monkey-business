I... I don't feel so good. I feel weird, or something.

  
 
 

What do you mean?
 

I don't know. I ate half of a leftover burrito out of the fridge, then left the wrapper on that guy Marty's desk. The guy whose burrito it was saw the wrapper and started yelling at Marty. Long story short, Marty wound up shoving the guy. He fell and cracked his head and had to go to the hospital. Marty got fired on the spot. He was crying as security marched him out the door. Actually crying. And I started to get this really... weird feeling. I don't like it.

  
 
 

Mortimer, I think that feeling is what the rest of us call "remorse."
