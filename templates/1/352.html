Original release date: Sun, 1 Jul 2012
 

How did you survive the Great Internet Outage of 2012?
 

Well, let's see... I read a book for the first half-hour or so. Then I called my Grandma, I hadn't talked to her in ages. My next-door neighbor needed help hanging some curtains, so I did that, and we wound up talking for a while. Turns out he's a really interesting guy. Used to live in Thailand - man, he had some stories. After that, I came home and folded some laundry that had been sitting there for two days. Then I went to bed early and got the first good night's sleep I've had in weeks.

  
 
 

Wow, you got a lot done.
 

I know, it was horrible! Let's hope it never happens again.
