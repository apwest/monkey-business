Original release date: Mon, 15 Oct 2012

It's not fair! Woot and Archie McPhee are giving away a bunch of awesome Halloween stuff this week in their Crap or Treat mini-sweepstakes, but you have to "Like" Woot's Facebook page to win any of it!

  
 
 

Well, you can't win anyway, because you work for Woot.
 

I'm just glad I found out about the Facebook thing before I turned in my resignation letter.

  
 
 

So, wait: you were willing to quit your job to try to win something in Crap or Treat, but you're not willing to join Facebook?
 

No, I'm banned from Facebook. I swear, you sell a few squid-gland supplements that send a couple of people to the hospital, and suddenly Facebook is judge, jury, and executioner.
