Original release date: Thu, 16 Feb 2012

As I've walked the campaign trail these past several hours, I've heard the deep concerns of the American people. I've heard a nation crying out for solutions.

  
 
 

Actually, that was me crying out for my phone charger.
 

And as I stand before you today, I pledge to you: A Mortimer administration will ensure that every American, no matter his or her station in life, has equal access to his or her coworkers' phone chargers.

  
 
 

So things around here won't change much, then.
