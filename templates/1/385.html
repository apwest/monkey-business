Man, every time I turn the Olympics on, it's the same thing! Crying gymnasts! Bikini volleyball! And the swimming, my God, the swimming! How many different races do they need? IT'S ALL SWIMMING!

  
 
 

What would you prefer?
 

Y'know, the weird sports! The ones everybody forgets about until the Olympics! Where's my water polo? My team handball? My shot-put? Do you realize that of all the swimmers I've seen in the past week, not two were synchronized?

  
 
 

OK, well, if you check the cable listings here, you can find all of that stuff. See, look. Here's some team handball on right now.
 

Oh. Uh, OK. Cool. I'll leave that on while I think up something else to complain about.
