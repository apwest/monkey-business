Original release date: Sun, 26 May 2013

I'm going to the big music festival this weekend, you coming?

 
 
 

Oh, you mean the one where you spend $500 to camp in a crowded mud-pit to listen to 12 straight hours of live music, only 3 of which you're able to actually catch because you spend the rest of the time waiting in line for the bathrooms and overpriced food carts?

 

Yup! So that's a soft yes?
