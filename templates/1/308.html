Original release date: Mon, 14 May 2012
 

Oh, boy. You're wearing a costume again. Still chasing that dream of being a superhero?
 

No, not anymore.

  
 
 

Then why the mask, and cape, and gloves, and boots, and underwear worn on the outside of your pants?
 

I've got my eyes set on a more realistic goal: superhero sidekick. You don't need any powers. You get to live in some rich guy's mansion rent-free. And you do things like start the car or call the cops while the superhero takes all the damage.

  
 
 

I've always suspected that you and I had different standards for "reality."
 

Now I just need to get adopted. Do you know any billionaires who seem like they might lead secret lives?
