Original release date: Mon, 26 Dec 2011
 

And now the post-holiday depression sets in. Back to the grind. Back to reality. Sigh.
 

Hey Monte -

  
 
 

Those glittering dreams of joy, hope, and brotherhood lie discarded like so much crumpled wrapping paper. The weather hasn't changed, but it feels so much colder now.
 

Monte! I'm trying to ask you if you want to go to lunch and help me spend this gift card from Pizza Trough.

  
 
 

Oh, of course I do! Thanks! But why didn't you say something before I wasted all that perfectly good moaning?
 

I'm sure there's plenty more where that came from. Now let's eat.
