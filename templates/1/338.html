Pretty crazy news about Matt Rutledge leaving Woot, huh?
 

Yeah, crazy... like a fox.

  
 
 

But that doesn't even make sense.
 

No, what doesn't make sense is making small talk while a power vacuum is waiting to be filled! In times of crisis, frightened people turn to a strong leader! Wooters are crying out for an iron paw to seize the helm! That's why I'm moving into Matt's office right now and refusing to leave until I'm granted dictatorial powers over the entire company!

  
 
 

OK, sure. But why do you have all those baggies?
 

The fight to restore dignity and honor and glory will be a long one! And let's just say my will is stronger than my bladder.
