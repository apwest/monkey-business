Original release date: Sat, 14 Jul 2012

You know what really burns me up? I mean, really really gets on my last nerve?

  
 
 

No, but -
 

When I'm trying to do something urgent, like catch a bus, and some self-centered loudmouth just won't shut up about some stupid, petty little point he's trying to make. Do they ever stop to consider that maybe, maybe, people have more important things to do? Oh, no. Not them. It's all about me, me, me.

  
 
 

Funny you should say that -
 

That's what's wrong with the world, if you ask me. Too many people with zero consideration for others. News flash, people: you're not the center of the universe! Oh, hey, you've got your backpack on. Where are you headed?

  
 
 

Oh, just to the bus stop to wait 40 minutes for the next bus.
