Original release date: Sat, 11 May 2013

Today I learned that I've been eating bananas the wrong way my entire life.

 
 
 

Oh yeah?

 

Yeah. According to proper etiquette, you're supposed to cut off the ends, slice it lengthwise on both sides, peel off the top with your flatware, and then cut off bite-sized pieces with the side of your fork.

 
 
 

How have you been doing it?

 

I usually just put the banana behind the tire of a car, and wait for the driver to back up until it shoots out of the peel.
